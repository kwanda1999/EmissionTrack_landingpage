Who Are We?

EmissionTrack is a consultancy specializing in emission data analysis and management, focusing on Scope 3 emissions and carbon footprinting. Our mission is to help clients achieve sustainability and reduce their environmental impact. We are dedicated to being a leading provider of emission data analysis and management services in South Africa and be recognized globally.

What We Here For

The call for global sustainability is stronger than ever, urging businesses to monitor and cut down on carbon emissions. Surprisingly, only 10% of companies, as per a Boston Consulting Group (BCG) survey, were tracking all their greenhouse gas emissions by 2022. Similarly, a Harvard Business Review article found that just one in 10 companies fully measured their GHG emissions at the same time. Despite the rising importance of emission tracking, it's concerning that many companies haven't taken comprehensive action. This not only affects transparency and efficiency but also undermines global climate change efforts.

What We Are Doing About It

Introducing our advanced Emission Tracking system, which incorporates real-time emissions monitoring through GPS and sensors, AI analysis for actionable insights on reducing environmental impact, easy-to-understand reports visualized using user-friendly platforms like Tableau, and secure data storage with blockchain technology to ensure integrity and reliability.
